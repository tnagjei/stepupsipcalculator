How Goal Planning Works

Goal Planning with SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) helps you determine how much you need to invest regularly to achieve your financial goals. The calculator takes into account:

  • Your target amount
  • Time period to achieve the goal
  • Expected return rate
  • Inflation rate
  • Initial investment amount
  • Compounding frequency

The formula used for calculating the required monthly SIP is:

PMT = (FV * r) / ((1 + r)^n - 1)

Where:

  • PMT = Required monthly investment
  • FV = Future value (target amount)
  • r = Monthly interest rate
  • n = Total number of months

The inflation-adjusted amount is calculated using:

Adjusted Amount = Target Amount * (1 + Inflation Rate)^Years
